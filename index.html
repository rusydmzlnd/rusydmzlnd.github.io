Who am I

I live with creativity

My name is Muhammad Rusydie bin Mudzland and I am 20 years old. Currently studying in Diploma of New Media Communication and Contentprenuership at UiTM Alor Gajah, Melaka. Back in my highschool days, I took Graphic Design in Computer and Arts as my elective subject which is why I decided to persued my studies in new media at UiTM. I also learnt a lot about a things I love the most which is photography in my school. After graduated, I started to take photos randomly with my camera and post it on my social media; Instagram. Then the covid hits. It took me a long time to refresh my photography skills in the post-pandemic event. My aim in life is to achive my dream to work as a web content creator in a company. My first choice would be a social media manager which means I can create a content and caption for the company's social media specifically Instagram. But I also have variables new media skill sets like making a 2D animation, video and photo editing, graphic designing and not to forget I can work as game level designer(my last choice).
